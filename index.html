1: PURPOSE

To establish standardized guidelines for safe and professional Air-1 operations.

2: Scope

Applies to all certified LSPD pilots assigned to Air-1.

3: Authority & Certification

Only members with LSPD Aviation Certification may pilot a helicopter.

4: General Rules

  • Maintain a professional and calm demeanor at all times.
  • No low flying over populated areas unless tactically necessary.
  • No hovering above civilians for extended periods.
  • No spotlighting or tracking without ground request or cause.
  • Announce: ‚ÄúAir-1 is up and in the air, and available.‚Äù
  • No surveillance on civilians without suspicion + ground request.
  • No landing in roads for no reason.

5: Radio Commutation Standards

  • Use 10-codes + phonetic alphabet.
  • Callouts include:
    • "Air-1 overhead [location], eyes on."
    • "Suspect heading [direction] at high speed."
    • "Vehicle is now FOOTER, heading [direction]."

6: Pursuit Support Protocol

  • Ground must request Air-1 first.
  • Air-1 becomes primary once overhead.
  • Provide direction, speed, and conditions.
  • Announce when suspect bails.
  • Break off if pursuit is terminated.

7: Thermal Usage

  • Thermals are only allowed when:
    • A REQUEST MUST BE MADE BEFORE USING THERMALS!
    • Suspect enters water (Must Request before using).
    • Clean chase is broken (only for the breaking vehicle)(Must Request before using).
    • Gang raids (Must Request before using).
    • Open-scene shootouts (Must Request before using).

Violating this SOP will lead to your Termination from the Aviation Unit.
