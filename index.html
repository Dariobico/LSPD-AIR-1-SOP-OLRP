LSPD Air-1 SOP

Home Sections Aviation Unit Contact Command

1. PURPOSE

To establish standardized guidelines for the safe, effective, and professional operation of LSPD Air-1 in support of ground units, pursuits, searches, surveillance, and emergency response within One Life.

2. SCOPE

Applies to all certified LSPD pilots assigned to Air-1.

3. AUTHORITY & CERTIFICATION

Only members with LSPD Aviation Certification (completed Air-1 training and check ride) may pilot a helicopter.

4. GENERAL RULES

  • Maintain a professional and calm demeanor on air and main radio channels.
  • No low flying over highly populated areas unless tactically necessary.
  • No hovering directly above civilians for extended periods.
  • No spotlighting or tracking civilians without cause.
  • Announce on duty: “Air-1 is up and available.”
  • No random civilian tracking without suspicion.
  • Do not land in the middle of the road without reason.

5. RADIO COMMUNICATION STANDARDS

  • Use proper phonetic alphabet and 10-codes.
  • “Air-1 is overhead [location] with eyes.”
  • “Suspect heading [direction] at high speed.”
  • “Vehicle is now FOOTER heading [direction].”

6. PURSUIT SUPPORT PROTOCOL

  • Ground unit must request Air-1.
  • Air-1 becomes primary when overhead.
  • Provide constant updates (speed, direction, traffic conditions).
  • Announce if suspect bails.
  • If ground calls off—Air-1 breaks off unless told otherwise.

Violating this SOP will lead to your Termination of the Aviation Unit.
